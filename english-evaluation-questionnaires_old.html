Evaluation Questionnaires

+ - Question - 1
1. A tract between two areas of the respiratory system
2. An abnormal opening between two areas of the body (usually the bladder and the vagina, but can also be rectum and vagina) which develops during the course of a prolonged/obstructed labor and birth
3. A hole in the heart because of trauma
4. An abnormal connection between an artery and vein.
+ - Question - 2
1. Delay in decision to seek care
2. Delay in reaching care
3. Delay in receiving adequate health care
4. All of the above
+ - Question - 3
1. Female genital mutilation
2. Sexual violence
3. Prolonged obstructed labor
4. Accidental injuries during surgery or episiotomy during childbirth
+ - Question - 4
1. Uncontrollable passing of the urine or feces
2. Smell during leakage of urine
3. Urinary tract infection
4. All of the above
+ - Question - 5
1. Providing health education to families on core topics such as family planning, antenatal care, institutional delivery, postnatal care and HIV.
2. Identifying obstetric fistula at the community level, counseling the woman and referring for care
3. Referring women to health centers for antenatal care and following-up with information about birth preparedness, complication readiness and warning signs of problems in pregnancy and childbirth
4. All of the above
